Meaning of Aran's flag

The green, white, and red are Iran’s national colors. Their presence in the flag signifies Aran's identity and belonging to the Iranian nation — a visual affirmation of cultural and historical connection.

At the center of the flag, the rising sun stands as a powerful symbol of awareness, truth, and the reawakening of historical consciousness. It represents the return of light after a dark night — a metaphor for overcoming hardship and reclaiming clarity and purpose.

Beneath the sun lies a blue line, symbolizing the Aras River — a river that has long marked a politically imposed boundary, not a natural or cultural divide. The placement of the rising sun just north of the Aras emphasizes that what lies beyond the river is not foreign, but part of the same heritage. The sun rising over the river is a quiet yet firm statement: awareness and belonging do not recognize imposed borders.
